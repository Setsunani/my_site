Biomes of the World

One of our secondary pages, "Biomes of the World," takes a deep dive into the incredible variety of natural environments our planet is home to. Here, visitors can learn about different biomes, from vast tropical forests to icy tundra. Each biome is presented with detailed information, spectacular images and notable facts. Our visitors can explore the diversity of plant and animal life in each biome and understand how these regions play a crucial role in the health of our planet.
